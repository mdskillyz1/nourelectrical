Questions before booking?

Clear answers before you call an electrician.

Do you offer emergency electrician callouts in London?

Yes. Call or WhatsApp with your postcode and the issue. If an electrician is available, Nour Electrical can advise on the next step and arrange urgent help.

How long does an EICR take?

Most domestic EICR inspections take around 2 to 4 hours depending on the size and condition of the property. Certificates are normally issued after the inspection and report are completed.

Can you help with landlord electrical work?

Yes. Nour Electrical can help landlords with inspections, remedial work, smoke alarms, consumer units and general rental property electrical jobs.

Can I send photos before booking?

Yes. Sending clear photos or a short video by WhatsApp often helps the electrician understand the fault and provide a faster estimate.
