What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences, improve performance, and understand how visitors use the site.

How this site uses cookies

Nour Electrical may use cookies to keep the website working, remember your cookie preference, understand basic website usage, and improve the customer experience.

Types of cookies used

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality, including remembering your cookie consent preference.
  • Analytics cookies: Optional cookies that may help us understand how visitors use the site, such as popular pages and general traffic patterns.
  • Communication cookies: Cookies or similar technologies may be used by third-party tools linked from the site, such as WhatsApp, Google, or form services.

Managing or disabling cookies

You can accept or reject optional cookies using the cookie banner. You can also manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect how parts of the website work.
