# SEO Service Page Template

Use this structure for each high-value service page.

## SEO Fields

Page title:
[Service] in London | Nour Electrical

Meta description:
Need [service] in London? Nour Electrical provides qualified electrical work for homes, landlords and businesses. Call or WhatsApp for a fast quote.

URL:
/services/[service-keyword-london]/

## Page Structure

H1:
[Service] in London

Opening section:
Explain the service, who it is for and why the customer should book Nour Electrical. Include London naturally in the first paragraph.

CTA:
Call 07862 632885 or WhatsApp a photo for a faster quote.

Trust section:
- Qualified electrician
- Clear pricing before work starts
- Certificates provided where required
- 12-month workmanship guarantee
- Domestic, commercial and landlord work

Service details:
Explain what is included, common problems solved and what the customer can expect on the visit.

Pricing guidance:
Give starting prices or explain what affects the quote. This builds trust even when exact prices vary.

Areas covered:
Mention London plus the priority service areas.

FAQs:
Add 4-6 specific questions for the service.

Final CTA:
Ready to book [service]? Call or WhatsApp Nour Electrical today.
